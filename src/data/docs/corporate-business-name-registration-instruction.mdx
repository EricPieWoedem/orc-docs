### Business Details

This section allows the applicant to provide detailed information about the corporate business name and other essential business particulars.

**Steps:**
- Enter the "Business Name" and confirm its availability.
- Select one or more International Standard Industrial Classification (ISIC) codes applicable to the business.
- Specify the "Principal Business Activity".
- Describe the "Nature of Business".
- Set the "Date of Commencement".

### Business Contact Details

- Enter the "Mobile Number(Primary)" with country code (e.g., "+233") and "Email" (e.g., "johndoe@gmail.com").
- Add optional "Additional Mobile Number" and "Fax" (e.g., "+233 333 555-1234").
- Provide the "Website of your business" (e.g., "sample-site.com").

![Start Application|2000X1238](/7.png)

### Details of Parent Company

- Enter the ORC number in the input field and click **Look Up*.
- Click the **Select Representative from "Entity Name"**.
- Select the prefered Individual.
- Scroll down and click **Submit**.

![Start Application|2000X1238](/8.png)

### Review and Submit
- Review all entered information for accuracy.
- Click **Proceed to Payment** once all required inputs are verified or **Edit Application** to make changes.

![Start Application|2000X1238](/9.png)

### Payment

After verification, the system will guide you to the payment gateway.

- Follow the prompts to make the payment.

- Click the **Pay Now** button to be redirected to the Ghana.gov portal for payment processing.

![Start Application|2000X1238](/11.png)

- After payment is complete navigate back to the system to confirm payment.
- Click **I have paid** button

![Start Application|2000X1238](/paid9.png)

- Upon successful payment and verification, the application will be processed, and you will be redirected to your dashboard where entity stays in drafts awaiting approval.

![Start Application|2000X1238](/12.png)

- Upon approval the entity's status becomes **Active**
