# Corporate Business Name

## Overview
A company may carry on business under a business name which does not consist of its corporate name. This is called a corporate business name . A stamp of the parent company is required and should be signed by one of its existing directors or secretary.

## Start Application
The "Start application" button allows users to begin the process of registering a corporate business. Clicking the button will guide users through submitting the necessary information and documents required for registration, based on the corporate business agreement.

![Start Application|2000X1238](/step1.png)

### How to register
- A search for the availability of the company name can be conducted by performing a name search.
- Complete registration by providing other relevant information in the stepwise process.
- Any information related to your business registration can be found on your portal.

Note: Mandatory renewal is required of a registered corporate business name each year at a fee.

Users must confirm possession of all these documents by checking the box labeled “I have all relevant documents” before clicking the Proceed button to continue the registration.

![Start Application|2000X1238](/2.png)

### Prerequisite Documents
This section outlines the essential documents required to proceed with the partnership registration.
- **Details of Parent Company** - You must prepare comprehensive information about the parent company.
- **Ghana Post GPS Address** – You must provide a valid Ghana Post GPS address for the location of your business.

Users must confirm possession of all these documents by checking the box labeled “I have all relevant documents” before clicking the Proceed button to continue the registration. This step ensures preparedness and helps streamline the process in the subsequent stages.
