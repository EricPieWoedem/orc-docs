# Subscribers (Step 5 of 7)

This section allows you to add all individuals or organizations who are subscribing to shares in the company.

## 📝 How to Add a Subscriber:

### ✅ Step 1: Click on the "Add Subscriber(s)" button.

📸 

### ✅ Step 2: A pop-up modal will appear asking:

**Is the subscriber a body corporate or an individual?**

Select Either:
- Body Corporate
- Individual

📸 
### ✅ Step 3: If you Select Body Corporate:

The form expands to request the following:

**➡️ Enter the ORC Number of the corporate entity:**  
Once a valid ORC number is entered, the system will automatically populate required details of potential representatives from the given coorporate ORC number in a dropdown form
eg ***(sampleName - GHA123456789-0 - example@gmail.com)***

**➡️ Select the appropriate representative to act on behalf of the corporate entity.**

📸 

### ✅ Step 3:  If you choose Individual:

The form expands to collect the following details:

**➡️ Identification Type (e.g., ECOWAS Card, TIN)**

**➡️ Identification Number**

**➡️ Email Address**

**➡️Phone Number**

📸 

### ✅ Step 5: Share Details for Each Subscriber:

**➡️ Allotted Shares**

- Equity Shares

- Preference Shares

**➡️ Consideration in Cash**

- Equity

- Preference

**➡️ Consideration Otherwise Than In Cash**

- Equity

- Preference

**➡️ Amount Remaining to Be Paid in Cash**

- Equity

- Preference

📸 

### ✅ Step 6: Click "Save" to add the subscriber or "Cancel" to discard.

### **📌 Note:** You can repeat this process to add additional subscribers as needed.
## ➡️ After completing this section, click "Save and Continue" to proceed to the next step: Benefical Owner (BO)
